Plants Stores & Greenhouses

During the spring and summer months, the West Kootenay's is lucky to house several amazing gardening centers. Unfortunately, during the rest of the year, we have considerably fewer options. Below is a list of the best seasonal centers. During the fall and winter month, stores such as Canadian Tire, Rona, Home Hardware and Walmart are great for potting soil, pots, fertilizer,soil ammendments, tools, lights and even some starter plants.

  • Georama Growers
  • Family Tree Gardening
  • Two Thumbs Up Gardening
  • Dig Garden Center
  • Morris Flowers & Garden Center
  • Casey's Greenhouses
